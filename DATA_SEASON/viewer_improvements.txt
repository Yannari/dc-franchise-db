VP VIEWER — IMPROVEMENT BACKLOG
================================
Gaps and polish issues in the Visual Player. Engine work that never
surfaces visually, navigation issues, screen quality problems.


── PRIORITY 1 — MISSING SCREENS ──────────────────────────────────────

[A] RELATIONSHIPS / ALLIANCE STATE SCREEN
Where: between Camp Life and Voting Plans.
No screen currently surfaces bond + alliance state.
What to show:
  - Current named alliances and their members, with who just joined/left
    this episode (ep.allianceRecruits / ep.allianceQuits)
  - Biggest bond shifts this episode (ep.bondChanges)
  - Detected betrayals (detectBetrayals output)
  - Makes all engine work (vote discovery, lost ally, recruitment) visible
Data: ep.bondChanges, ep.allianceRecruits, ep.allianceQuits,
      ep.gsSnapshot.namedAlliances
Priority: HIGH — without this screen, most of the relationship engine
  is invisible to the viewer. Bond changes, betrayals, and alliance
  recruitment all fire but never appear as a dedicated VP moment.

[B] MERGE ANNOUNCEMENT SCREEN
The merge has no dedicated dramatic screen. Currently falls into the
first Camp Life block with no fanfare.
What to show:
  - Full merged cast portrait grid (new tribe banner + tribe color)
  - Top alliance threats going into merge
  - Who's on the bottom / currently unallied
Condition: ep.isMerge === true
Insert: between Tribe Status and Camp Life on merge episodes
Priority: HIGH — merge is the biggest structural moment in the game.
  It currently gets no visual treatment of its own.

[C] RI DUEL SCREEN
ep.riDuel has winner/loser/context data but no VP screen exists.
The duel just disappears into episode history.
What to show:
  - Two players, portrait pair, duel result
  - Who returns to game / who is finally eliminated
  - Similar structure to the Challenge screen
Condition: ep.riDuel exists
Priority: MEDIUM — only relevant when RI is active, but when it is,
  the duel is a major story beat with no visual representation.


[H] WHY THIS VOTE HAPPENED SCREEN (rpBuildAftermath)
Exists but has known gaps — engine work fires that never surfaces here.
Known missing:
  - Comfort blindside flag: if ep.comfortBlindspotPlayer === ep.eliminated,
    a note should appear explaining they were spotted checked out pre-tribal
    (the Tribal screen has this, Aftermath doesn't mirror it)
  - Idol misplay ally analysis: tipOffAlly section shows ally intuition stat
    but doesn't note when the ally was in a rival alliance (isRivalTipOff)
  - Alliance recruitment: if ep.allianceRecruits fired this episode, no
    mention in WHY of who just got absorbed and what it means for the vote
Data: ep.comfortBlindspotPlayer, ep.idolMisplays, ep.allianceRecruits,
      ep.tipOffBetrayalFired
Priority: MEDIUM — screen exists, just incomplete. Gaps make the post-vote
  analysis feel like it's missing context the engine already has.

[I] VOTING PLANS SCREEN GAPS
Screen exists but some newer systems don't feed into it.
Known missing:
  - No visual for comfort blindspot players in the plans — they're the top
    target but show no "checked out" signal in the plan cards
  - Defection log (ep.defections) not surfaced — swing voters who broke
    from their bloc mid-vote have no callout before the reveal
Data: ep.comfortBlindspotPlayer, ep.defections, ep.alliances
Priority: LOW — polish, but these are real signals the engine tracks.


── PRIORITY 2 — NAVIGATION PROBLEMS ──────────────────────────────────

[D] SIDEBAR GETS NOISY (3+ TRIBES)
With 3 tribes you get: "Yellow Camp", "Blue Camp", "Red Camp",
"Yellow — After TC", "Blue — After TC", "Red — After TC" — all same
style, no grouping, impossible to scan.
Fix:
  - Add a color dot matching tribe color to each camp sidebar label
    (tribeColor() already exists, just thread it into the sidebar label)
  - Group pre-camp and post-camp with a subtle divider or indent level
Location: sidebar labels are built in the vpScreens.push() block inside
  the main VP builder (search for `id:'camp-`). Each push has a `label`
  string — that's where the color dot goes.
Priority: MEDIUM — noticeable on any 3-tribe pre-merge episode.

[E] DUPLICATE "TWISTS & EVENTS" LABEL
Both pre-tribal twist screen and post-elimination twist screen use the
same sidebar label. Can't tell them apart.
Fix:
  - Pre-tribal twist  → "Pre-Tribal Events" (or keep "Twists & Events")
  - Post-elim twist   → "After Tribal" or "Post-Vote Twist"
Priority: LOW — cosmetic but causes confusion when navigating.


── PRIORITY 3 — SCREEN QUALITY ────────────────────────────────────────

[F] CAMP LIFE OVERWHELMING POST-MERGE
Post-merge, all active players' events land in one block with no
hierarchy. Long scroll, no way to filter.
Options:
  - Group events by type (strategy / social / confessional)
  - Show player-specific "standout moment" callout for players with
    confessional-type events — prioritise those
  - Cap to N events with an expand button
Priority: MEDIUM — becomes worse as game goes late and more events fire.

[G] COLD OPEN IS STATIC
Shows cast status (alive/eliminated) but no narrative hook. Feels like
a roster check, not an episode opener.
What to add:
  - "Coming in from last episode": who's on the bottom, lingering
    threats, top alliance going into this ep
  - Pull from prevEp.gsSnapshot bond data + last episode's
    allianceQuits / allianceRecruits
Priority: LOW — polish. Makes every episode of VP feel like a story,
  not just a roster dump. Especially valuable from episode 3+ when
  the bond/alliance history has something to pull from.

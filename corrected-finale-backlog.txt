=== CAMP EVENTS ===
- The Last Morning
Day 51. The fire is low. The game is almost over.
Bowie — Mastermind — 1 win
Jasmine — Perceptive Player — 4 wins
Bowie: "I came into this game knowing exactly what I wanted to do. Control the votes. Control the relationships. Control the outcome. And I'm still here — so either I did that, or I got lucky. I don't believe in luck."
He built his game around Carrie and Mickey. Some of those bonds survived. Some didn't. Zoey was the rival he never shook.
Bowie has survived 10 votes against. The target was always there.
Jasmine: "People underestimated me early. They saw a physical player and thought that's all I had. But I learned. I adapted. And every time they came for me, I won the challenge that mattered."
She built hers game around Priya and Josee. Some of those bonds survived. Some didn't. Carrie was the rival she never shook.
Jasmine has dominated challenges. The jury knows it.

- The sun rises on the last day. The camp feels different. Smaller. The empty spots where the others used to sit say more than anyone will today.
- Bowie sits alone, running the numbers one last time. Every vote, every alliance, every move that led here. "I made this happen. Today I finish it."
- Jasmine takes a walk around camp. Past the shelter she built. Past the fire pit where every real conversation happened. "I'm going to miss this place. Not the game — the people."
- Bowie catches Jasmine's eye across camp. No words. Just the understanding that one of them is about to end the other's game. For good.
- They walk out of camp single file. Nobody looks back. The game is ahead of them now, not behind.

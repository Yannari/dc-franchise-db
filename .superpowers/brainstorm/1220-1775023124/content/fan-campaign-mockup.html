B — Campaign Trail

Three-phase structure: The Pitch (speeches), Jury Reacts (jury commentary — they give their take but don't vote), Final Plea (last words to camera). Shows running popularity bars. Jury's opinions inform the audience but don't decide.

Both options include:

  • Personality-driven speeches (bold/strategic/social variants)
  • Jury present as commentators, not voters
  • Fan reaction elements (social media feel)
  • Popularity data visible but not final tallies (save suspense for the Vote screen)

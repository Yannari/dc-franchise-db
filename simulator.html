SEASON BASICS
FORMATS & TWISTS
Tiebreaker Mode ▼

Survivor: tied players can't vote on the revote; if still tied, non-tied players draw rocks. Total Drama/DC: tied players compete head-to-head in a physical or puzzle challenge — loser is eliminated, no rocks, no revote.

Firemaking Final Elimination ▼

At the last bloodfire before the finale, the immunity winner picks 2 players to duel in a firemaking challenge. Non-picked players earn Shared Immunity. Loser is eliminated.

2nd Chance Isle Format ▼

Voted-out players go to 2nd Chance Isle instead of leaving. They duel each episode — the winner stays. One player re-enters the game.

Shot in the Dark ▼

Any player at any tribal can sacrifice their vote for a 1-in-6 chance at safety. If safe, all votes cast against them are nullified.

Journey Format ▼

Select players embark on a journey each episode. They gamble their vote for a chance at an advantage. If they don't gamble, they lose their vote.

Fan Decided Format ▼

Let fans vote on key format decisions — tribes, merge timing, jury size, twists. You can still override anything after.

  Trigger: schedulable, post-challenge (pre-merge only).
  Mechanic: winning tribe raids losing camp. Takes food/comfort/tools. Bond damage
    toward raiders. Creates cross-tribal grudges. Works with survival mechanics.

[TD-4] CHOOSE YOUR CHAMPION
  Trigger: schedulable, replaces tribe challenge (pre-merge only).
  Mechanic: each tribe picks 1v1 champion. Win = hero moment. Lose = blame.
    Creates "why didn't you pick me?" drama.

  Mechanic: captains draft teams. Last picked = bottom. Creates pecking order.
  Note: overlaps with tribe-swap. Lower priority than Raid/Champion.

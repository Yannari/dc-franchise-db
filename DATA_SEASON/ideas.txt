POPULARITY SYSTEM — DONE
========================
Fan vote twist + fan favorite end-game award.
Brainstormed 2026-03-24. Implemented 2026-03-25.


── OVERVIEW ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

A persistent per-player popularity score (gs.popularity[name]) that accumulates
across all episodes via event-driven deltas. Drives two features:

  1. Fan Vote Twist — highest-popularity player gets saved (pre-merge: immunity,
     post-merge: advantage)
  2. Fan Favorite Award — announced after FTC, pure bragging rights + VP screen


── STATE ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

gs.popularity[name] — running integer, starts at 0, never resets.
Eliminated players keep their final score (used for fan favorite).
Saved to ep.gsSnapshot each episode.

Three axes with per-episode caps to prevent runaway scores:

  Drama    — max +6 per episode
  Likability — max +4 per episode
  Underdog — max +5 per episode

Caps are per-axis per-episode. A player can earn up to +15 in a single
standout episode across all three axes.

── FAN VOTE TWIST ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Engine type: 'fan-vote-boot' (already exists, needs replacement logic)

Current behavior: random weighted by low social + high boldness.
New behavior: saves the HIGHEST popularity player instead.

Trigger: twist fires as scheduled in the twist calendar.

Resolution:
  - Find highest gs.popularity score among active players.
  - Pre-merge: that player receives full tribal immunity this episode.
  - Post-merge: that player receives an extra vote advantage
    (added to gs.advantages, usable at any future tribal).
  - If multiple players are tied for highest: tiebreak by earliest episode
    appearance (longer survivor = more fan investment).

Twist narrative (VP):
  - Dedicated screen showing saved player portrait.
  - Their score + top 2 reasons they earned it (most recent delta sources).
  - "Fans have spoken — [Name] is safe." / "Fans have spoken — [Name]
    receives an Extra Vote."
  - Badge: FAN VOTE SAVE (gold)


── FAN FAVORITE AWARD ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Fires after FTC winner is revealed. No game effect — title + VP screen only.

Winner: player with highest gs.popularity score at season end.
  - Active players AND eliminated players are both eligible.
  - If the game winner also has the highest score: combined card
    ("Winner AND Fan Favorite").

VP screen content:
  - Portrait of fan favorite player.
  - Title card: FAN FAVORITE — [Name]
  - Their episode-by-episode popularity arc (ep1: +2, ep2: +5, ep4: +4…)
  - The single biggest moment that defined their arc (highest delta event).
  - If eliminated: "Eliminated [ep.X] — but never forgotten."


── VP VIEWER INTEGRATION ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Cold Open screen:
  - "Fan Pulse" row added below the cast roster.
  - Shows top 3 players by current score with a small rank indicator.

Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH — builds on existing event infrastructure.

SURVIVAL MECHANICS — PLANNED
=============================
Food & water system. UI exists (cfg-food-water, cfg-survival-difficulty) but no engine.
Brainstormed 2026-03-27.


── OVERVIEW ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Per-player survival score (gs.survival[name]) that decays each episode.
Low survival creates challenge penalties, emotional pressure, camp conflict,
and at brutal difficulty, medevac risk. Makes reward challenges meaningful.


── STATE ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

gs.survival[name] — 0-100, starts at 100. Decays per episode based on difficulty.
Saved to ep.gsSnapshot each episode.

Difficulty decay rates (per episode):
  Casual:    -3 to -5 (slow, survival is flavor)
  Realistic: -6 to -10 (noticeable, food matters)
  Brutal:    -10 to -16 (harsh, Koh-Lanta style)

Variance: +(endurance * 0.3) reduces decay (fit players handle it better).


── EFFECTS OF LOW SURVIVAL ───────────────────────────────────────────────────

Threshold    Effect
---------    ------
< 70         Mild challenge penalty (-0.5 to stat rolls)
< 50         Moderate penalty (-1.0), emotional push toward desperate/paranoid
< 35         Severe penalty (-1.5), camp conflict events (food fights, hoarding)
< 20         Critical: -2.0 penalty, medevac risk (brutal only, ~8% per episode)

Medevac: player is removed from the game (not voted out, no jury).
Dramatic VP screen showing medical team, player's reaction, tribe impact.


── WHAT RESTORES SURVIVAL ────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Source                          Restore amount
------------------------------  --------
Reward challenge win (Feast)    +25
Reward challenge win (Supplies) +15
Reward challenge win (Overnight)+20
Merge feast                     +30 (all players)
Fishing/foraging camp event     +5 to +10 (endurance-scaled)
Comfort reward                  +10
Auction: Full Meal              +20
Auction: Assorted Snacks        +10

Camp events that restore survival also have bond consequences (shared meals
bring people together, hoarding creates enemies).


── CAMP EVENTS (NEW) ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Low survival triggers new event types:
  - foodConflict: two players fight over rations (bond -1.5, both lose -1 survival)
  - hoarding: one player caught stashing food (bond -2.0 with discoverer)
  - fishing: high-endurance player catches food (+8 survival for tribe, +1.0 bond)
  - foraging: player finds fruit/coconuts (+5 survival, +0.5 bond with sharer)
  - starvationBond: two players bond over shared suffering (+1.0 bond)
  - rationing: strategic player manages food distribution (+0.5 bond with tribe)
  - collapse: player collapses from exhaustion (foreshadows medevac at brutal)

All events MUST have consequences (bond changes, survival changes, state changes).


── REWARD CHALLENGE INTEGRATION ──────────────────────────────────────────────

Currently most rewards (Feast, Comfort, Supply Cache) are pure flavor text.
With survival enabled, they restore survival points — making reward challenges
matter mechanically. Players on a losing streak get weaker in challenges AND
more emotionally volatile, creating a death spiral that only a reward win breaks.

This also makes the Survivor Auction more strategic: do you spend on food
(+20 survival) or gamble on a blind bid advantage?


── IMPLEMENTATION NOTES ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

- gs.survival initialized in initGame() as { [name]: 100 } for all players
- updateSurvival(ep) called at end of each episode alongside updatePlayerStates
- Reads cfg.foodWater ('disabled'|'enabled') and cfg.survivalDifficulty
- If disabled, function returns immediately (zero impact on existing games)
- Challenge stat functions receive a survivalPenalty modifier when survival < 70
- generateCampEventsForGroup checks tribe avg survival for food-related events
- Medevac: replaces normal elimination flow (no vote, no jury, dramatic exit)

Priority: MEDIUM — significant feature, touches challenge + camp + emotional systems.
Blocked by: nothing. Can be built incrementally (decay first, then events, then medevac).

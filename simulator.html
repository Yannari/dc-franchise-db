SEASON BASICS
FORMATS & TWISTS
Tiebreaker Mode ▼

Survivor: tied players can't vote on the revote; if still tied, non-tied players draw rocks. Total Drama/DC: tied players compete head-to-head in a physical or puzzle challenge — loser is eliminated, no rocks, no revote.

2nd Chance Isle Format ▼

Voted-out players go to 2nd Chance Isle instead of leaving. Format determines how the island works.

Shot in the Dark ▼

Any player at any tribal can sacrifice their vote for a 1-in-6 chance at safety. If safe, all votes cast against them are nullified.

Black Vote ▼

Every eliminated player (until F4) casts a secret vote or gift before leaving. Classic: extra vote against their enemy at next tribal. Modern: Extra Vote advantage to their closest ally.

Aftermath Show ▼

Aftermath: Chris interviews eliminated players, reveals secrets, shows unseen footage. Fan Vote: when enabled, the Aftermath includes a fan vote — the winner returns at the start of the next episode. No need to schedule second-chance separately.

Romance ▼

Romance: showmances, love triangles, and secret affairs. Disable for a purely strategic season.

Journey Format ▼

Select players embark on a journey each episode. They gamble their vote for a chance at an advantage. If they don't gamble, they lose their vote.

Exile Island Format ▼

Each episode, the winning tribe sends one member of the losing tribe to Exile Island to search for advantages. The exiled player returns for tribal — they are NOT safe.

Fan Decided Format ▼

Let fans vote on key format decisions — tribes, merge timing, jury size, twists. You can still override anything after.

SETTINGS & MECHANICS
Hidden Immunity Idol Settings ▼

Each tribe starts with a hidden idol. When played at tribal, all votes against the holder are cancelled.

When an idol is played, the tribe gets a new one to find.

Advantage Settings ▼

When this many players remain, all advantages will be removed. Set to 0 to disable.

Other Advantages
Quits, Expulsions & Medevacs ▼

When enabled, quits, expulsions, and medical evacuations may occur during the season.

Popularity System ▼

Tracks fan popularity across the season. Drives the Fan Vote twist (saves the highest-popularity player) and the Fan Favorite award after the Final Tribal Council.

Hides the Fan Pulse leaderboard during the season. Popularity still tracks internally — results only revealed at the Fan Vote finale.

Survival Mechanics ▼

Disabled: No food or water tracking. Players have unlimited resources.

Automatically adds a reward challenge every episode (stops at F4). Rewards restore tribe food — makes survival meaningful.

When a player is medically evacuated, the most recently voted-out player returns to take their spot. Incompatible with Redemption/Rescue Island.

Winning tribe can invite one person from the losing tribe to share the reward. Builds cross-tribal bonds and strategy.

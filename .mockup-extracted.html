The Final ChallengeVOLCANO
After a season spanning three continents, eleven flights, and one talking sock-puppet, the road ends here — on the slopes of an active Hawaiian volcano. The Final Three must first settle a tie. The winner of that joust faces the front-runner in a sacrificial-dummy race up the mountain. Whoever throws their dummy into the crater first takes home the million dollars. Lava, sharks, lawyers, ex-contestants, and at least one feral former champion stand in the way.
"Three of you walked off that plane. Only one is leaving with a check. Welcome to Hawaiian Punch." — your host, smiling too wide

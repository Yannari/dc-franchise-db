FUTURE: Universal Challenge Throw Mechanic
===========================================
Add "intentional underperformance" system to ALL twist challenges (not just generic).

Options explored:
A) Silent Throw — proportional chance (loyalty × bond strength) to quietly tank score.
   High-intuition teammates detect it post-hoc, generates heat.
B) Visible Dilemma — narrative beat with internal conflict text, stat-driven outcome.
   Witnesses form opinions, bonds shift. Best VP/text content.
C) Escalating Suspicion — first throw invisible, repeated throws accumulate suspicion
   like Mole system. Teammates call it out, "traitor" subplot.

Trigger conditions:
- Player has showmance on opposing tribe (pre-merge)
- Player promised alliance member on other side they'd help
- Player is being manipulated/blackmailed (social manipulation tie-in)

Recommendation: B (Visible Dilemma) for narrative richness, with C's accumulation
as a season-long layer on top.

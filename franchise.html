Franchise Evolution

Season 1 established the foundation with 24 new players and hidden immunity idols. Lindsay's social mastery won over Alejandro's strategic gameplay.

Season 2 brought back 14 returnees for intense warfare. Duncan's survival story beat Alejandro's immunity dominance (5 wins!). The season proved strategy alone doesn't guarantee victory - the jury wanted blood and struggle.

Season 3 introduced Gen 2 with 16 new players. Emma's perfect game (0 votes until F4) won in a tiebreaker. Scott's 6 challenge wins couldn't overcome Emma's loyalty strategy.

Season 4 combined All-Stars (9 Gen 1, 9 Gen 2) with Edge of Extinction. Alejandro FINALLY won his crown on his 3rd finals appearance, defeating Zoey's unprecedented 3 unbreakable bonds with strategic mastery. The closest finale ever (7-6-1) proved that in modern gameplay, strategy defeats pure social bonds.

Season 5 introduced 18 new players and a Survivor-style twist gauntlet (3 tribes, swap, split-tribal merge round, vote-loss twists, and Final 4 fire-making).
